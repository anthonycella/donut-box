flexbox cheat sheet


Note: these are all properties that are to be added to css selectors for tags, classes, ids etc.



1. Change your selector to use the flexbox display style
  display: flex

2. Change position of content on the x axis
  justify-content: [choice]
    [choices]
      - flex-start (auto): centers content to the left side of the element
      - center: centers content to the center of the element
      - flex-end: centers content to the right side of the element
      - space-between: spaces content evently on the x axis with no space between the outer elements and
        the sides of the box
      - space-evenly: spaces content evenly on the x axis with even space between the outer elements and
        the sides of the box
      - space-around: spaces content somewhere between space-between and space-evenly


2. Change position of content on the y axis
  justify-content: [choice]
    [choices]
      - flex-start (auto): centers content to the top of the element
      - center: centers content to the center of the y axis of the element
      - flex-end: centers content to the bottom of the element
      - space-between: spaces content evently on the y axis with no space between the outer elements and
        the sides of the box
      - space-evenly: spaces content evenly on the y axis with even space between the outer elements and
        the sides of the box
      - space-around: spaces content somewhere between space-between and space-evenly
